    Tomonari Kitahara¶

    I am an assistant professor at Department of Industrial Engineering and Economics, School of Engineering, Tokyo Institute of Technology.

    ¶

    Research Areas¶

    • Mathematical Programming, Linear Programming, The Simplex Method

    Curriculum Vitae¶

    Education¶

    • Mar. 2009: Doctor of Engineering from Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, Graduate School of Decision Science and Technology, Tokyo Institute of Technology.
    • Mar. 2006: Master of Engineering from Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, Graduate School of Decision Science and Technology, Tokyo Institute of Technology.
    • Mar. 2004: Bachelor of Engineering from Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Tokyo Institute of Technology.
